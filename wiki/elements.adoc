= elements

- `c` - character
- `p` or `>` - paragraph
- `b` or `*` - bold
- `i` or `/` - italic
- `u` or `_` - underline
- `s` or `~` - strikethrough
- `h1` or `=` - header 1
- `h2` or `==` - header 2
- `h3` or `===` - header 3
- `h4` or `====` - header 4
- `h5` or `=====` - header 5
- `h6` or `======` - header 6
- `m` or `{backtick}` - monospace
- `ul` or `-` - unordered list item
- `ol` or `.` - ordered list item
